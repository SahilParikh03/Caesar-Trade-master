2. Core Value Propositions

The terminal is built to solve three primary market needs:
A. Arbitrage Discovery & Execution

Different platforms often price the same event differently due to varying liquidity pools and user demographics.

    The Goal: Provide a side-by-side "Unified Order Book" that highlights price discrepancies in real-time.

    The Outcome: Enable traders to "lock in" risk-free or low-risk profits by buying on one exchange and selling on the other simultaneously.

B. Professional Market Scanning

Prediction markets are fragmented and move based on breaking news.

    The Goal: A high-density dashboard that aggregates news feeds, volume spikes, and price action across both venues.

    The Outcome: Users can identify emerging trends (e.g., a sudden shift in election odds or economic data) faster than the retail public, allowing for "first-mover" advantage.

C. Institutional Hedging & Risk Management

Traditional financial institutions and market makers need reliable tools to hedge real-world risks (interest rates, CPI, geopolitical events) using prediction markets.

    The Goal: Provide professional order types (Limit, Stop-Loss, Slippage Guards) and precise exposure monitoring.

    The Outcome: Large-scale participants can use prediction markets as a legitimate "signal engine" or insurance policy for their broader portfolios.
